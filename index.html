A Compassionate Path to Understanding

With over 20 years of clinical experience, Dr. Marie Haddox, Ph.D. has dedicated her practice to creating a holistic sanctuary where clients feel seen, heard, and deeply understood. Her commitment is rooted in the belief that a safe, therapeutic relationship is the foundation of all meaningful healing.

Dr. Haddox brings a wealth of expertise from her tenure at Banner Desert Medical Center and her advanced clinical training. She earned her Bachelor of Science from Arizona State University, followed by her Master's and Ph.D. from USIU San Diego.

At Spectrum Counseling, we navigate the complexities of modern identity, relationship dynamics, and family structures with an evidence-based yet intuitive approach.
