About Marie Haddox, Ph.D.

With over 20 years of clinical experience, Dr. Marie Haddox has dedicated her practice to creating a holistic sanctuary where clients feel seen, heard, and deeply understood. Her commitment is rooted in the belief that a safe, therapeutic relationship is the foundation of all meaningful healing.

Dr. Haddox brings a wealth of expertise from her tenure at Banner Desert Medical Center and her advanced clinical training. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Arizona State University, followed by her Master's and Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from USIU San Diego.

At Spectrum Counseling, we navigate the complexities of modern identity, relationship dynamics, and family structures with an evidence-based yet intuitive approach.

Expert Specialization

LGBTQ+ Affirming Therapy

We provide a deeply affirming space for the LGBTQ+ community. Dr. Haddox specializes in gender identity, transitioning support, and the unique challenges faced by queer individuals and couples. You don't have to explain your identity here—you are celebrated for it.

Therapy Specializations

Deep clinical expertise in depression, anxiety, relationships, sexuality, and gender identity counseling.

Depression Treatment

We work with individuals experiencing persistent sadness, loss of motivation, and emotional numbness. Dr. Haddox uses cognitive-behavioral and psychodynamic approaches to uncover root causes and develop sustainable coping strategies.

Anxiety & Stress

From generalized anxiety to panic disorders and social anxiety, we provide evidence-based treatment integrating mindfulness techniques with traditional therapeutic methods to help you regain control and build resilience.

Relationships & Couples

Strengthening connections, resolving conflict, and rebuilding intimacy. Therapy for all relationship structures, including couples and polyamorous/non-monogamous.

Sexuality

We offer a judgment-free space to discuss sexual orientation, intimacy concerns, and desire. Support includes coming out, navigating relationships, and building fulfilling intimate connections.

Gender Identity & Transition Support

We support clients questioning, exploring, or affirming their gender identity — including those considering or undergoing transition. Dr. Haddox is a member of the World Professional Association for Transgender Health (WPATH) and provides letters of support, guidance through social and medical transitions, and a deeply informed therapeutic space.

Insurance & Billing

Insurance & Payment at Spectrum Counseling

Dr. Haddox is considered out-of-network for all insurance plans and does not submit claims directly to insurance companies. However, we can provide a diagnosis and procedure code to help you submit a claim for potential out-of-network reimbursement. See our contact page for full rate and payment details.

Getting Started

Your First Session with Dr. Haddox

The initial consultation is an intake session where we discuss your history, goals, and determine if our practice is the right fit for your needs. This session typically lasts 60 minutes, and there's no pressure — it's a conversation to help us both understand how we can work together most effectively.

Session Details

How Long Are Counseling Sessions?

All sessions — individual, couples, and family — are 60 minutes. Extended sessions can be arranged for intensive therapeutic work when needed.

Virtual Care

Telehealth Therapy Across Arizona

Yes, we offer secure telehealth sessions for clients throughout Arizona. Virtual sessions follow the same structure as in-person appointments and are conducted through a HIPAA-compliant platform. Many clients find the flexibility of telehealth helpful for maintaining consistency in their care.

Policies

Our Cancellation Policy

We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand that life happens and will always work with you when unexpected circumstances arise.
